Privacy Policy

Jules Vigy Development does not directly collect or store any personally identifiable information about who uses our apps. We get our feedback and statistics from Apple Analytics. We use Apple Analytics for general statistics such as daily active players. Any game progress our apps save is only accessibles by you on your device and/or your Apple account. We do not collect any personal information. Anonymous and aggregated data may be collected to help us improve our apps. No ads will be run so that gives little incentive to collect extensive data on users.
